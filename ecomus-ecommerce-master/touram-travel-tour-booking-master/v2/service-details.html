Ziplining in Shnongpdeng, Dawki

Experience the thrill of ziplining over the crystal-clear waters of the Umngot River in Shnongpdeng, Dawki. This exhilarating activity offers breathtaking panoramic views of the lush green hills and the pristine river below. Glide through the air and feel the rush as you take on one of Meghalaya's most exciting adventure sports. The picturesque village of Shnongpdeng, located 8 kilometers north of Dawki on the Umngot River, is renowned for its natural beauty and adventure opportunities. The Umngot River, with its transparent waters, allows you to clearly see the depths and count the stones beneath. This activity is perfect for thrill-seekers and nature lovers alike, promising an unforgettable experience.

Whether you're a first-timer or an experienced adventurer, our certified guides ensure a safe and memorable ziplining experience. Equipped with top-notch safety gear and a thorough briefing, you'll be ready to soar across the valley. Beyond the adrenaline, the views are simply mesmerizing a perfect blend of adventure and scenic beauty. Don't miss this chance to create incredible memories in one of India's hidden gems!

About the Activity

Activity Location Activity Duration Activity Level
Shnongpdeng, Dawki, Meghalaya 20 Minutes (approx.) Medium

The picturesque village of Shnongpdeng is located 8 kilometers north of Dawki on the Umngot River. During the months of November to May, the Umngot River is known for its transparent water, allowing you to clearly see the depths and count the stones below. The activities at Shnongpdeng are available throughout the year, but the best time to visit for clear water views is during the dry season.

Ziplining is an adventure activity where you glide across a valley or river using a suspended wire. Participants wear a harness and are attached to the wire via a pulley, allowing them to safely traverse from one point to another at high speeds. This activity provides an adrenaline rush combined with spectacular aerial views.

Know before you book

  • Please adhere to the instructor's directions.
  • To prevent harming the equipment, use it with caution.
  • Make sure you're dressed comfortably.
  • Drugs and beverages bought from outside are not authorized.
  • Inside, no alcoholic beverages are permitted.

Participants must be above 10 years of age and weigh between 40kg to 110kg. Any medical conditions should be disclosed prior to the activity. It is recommended to bring sun protection and wear comfortable, athletic clothing. Keep your valuables secure or leave them at the designated storage areas.

Things to Carry

  • Swimwear
  • Comfortable Shoes
  • Sunscreen
  • Mobile
  • Camera
  • UA Teacher

*Note: Please ensure all personal items are securely stored during the activity to prevent loss.

It's also advisable to bring a small waterproof bag for essentials like your phone and wallet. A towel and a change of clothes will be useful after the activity, especially if you engage in water sports.

How To Reach

By Air:

The nearest Airport is Shillong Airport (SHL) which is around 100 KM away from the destination. One can hire a cab or take a bus from the airport. Alternatively, Guwahati International Airport (GAU) is a larger hub, approximately 180 KM away, offering more flight options. From Guwahati, you can hire a private taxi or take a shared sumo/bus to Dawki/Shnongpdeng.

By Rail:

The nearest railway station is Guwahati Railway Station (GHY) which is around 170 KM away. You can take a City car or a cab for Shillong, and then take a local cab to Dawki. From Shillong, local shared taxis are frequently available to Dawki/Shnongpdeng.

By Road:

The nearest bus station is Shillong (SHL) from where you can take a cab to Shillong. Shared taxis can then be hailed to reach Dawki from Shillong. Direct buses from Guwahati also run to Dawki, offering a more direct route. The road conditions are generally good, providing a scenic journey.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the cost for Ziplining in Shnongpdeng?
The cost for Ziplining in Shnongpdeng varies based on the package. The basic package, which includes 20 minutes of Ziplining and safety equipment, is 699 per person. Other packages with additional activities will have different prices.
When Ziplining in Shnongpdeng, how is safety ensured?
Safety is paramount. All participants are provided with high-quality safety harnesses, helmets, and gloves. Our experienced and certified instructors conduct a thorough safety briefing before the activity and are present throughout to ensure all safety protocols are strictly followed. Equipment is regularly inspected and maintained.
What is the best time to visit Shnongpdeng for Ziplining?
The activities at Shnongpdeng are available throughout the year. However, for the clearest water views of the Umngot River, the best time to visit is during the dry months, typically from November to May. The weather during these months is also pleasant for outdoor activities.
Are there any age or weight restrictions for ziplining?
Yes, participants must typically be above 10 years of age. There is also a weight restriction, usually between 40 kg to 110 kg, for safety reasons. It's always best to confirm the exact restrictions with the activity provider upon booking.
What other activities are available in Shnongpdeng besides ziplining?
Besides ziplining, Shnongpdeng offers a variety of adventure activities including boating/kayaking in the Umngot River, cliff jumping, snorkeling, and camping. Some packages combine ziplining with other activities for a complete adventure experience.
